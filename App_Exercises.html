Scales

  • Chromatic Scale: Practice both up-down and down-up. Strike every note on the xylo, focusing on creating a sustained, connected sound.
  • Bursts: Small bursts of 3-6 chromatic notes both up or down, starting on any note. Checks your ability to throw out fast notes using relaxed movements.
  • Major scales: Two octaves, up and down, no stopping or rolls.
  • Major Diads: One octave, up and down, no stopping or rolls. Practice 3rds and octaves, then 6ths and 10ths
